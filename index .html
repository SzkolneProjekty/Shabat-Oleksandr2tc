Difference between AR and VR

Augmented Reality (AR)

AR overlays digital information on the real world. It enhances the real-world environment with images, sounds, and other sensory inputs using devices like smartphones, tablets, or AR glasses.

Virtual Reality (VR)

VR immerses users in a completely virtual environment. Using VR headsets, users can experience a simulated world that feels real, often used in gaming, simulations, and training environments.
